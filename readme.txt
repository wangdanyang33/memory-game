This is a card matching game

Rules and Regulations:
You have to match cards with identical face.
Your moves are counted out as you proceed the game.
If your moves are equal to 20 then you will get 3 Stars.
If your moves are greater than 20 and less than 30 then you will get 2 Stars.
Else you will get 1 Star.
You can also restart the game at any stage by clicking the restart button.
